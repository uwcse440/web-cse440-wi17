The Problem

People traveling to different countries often have troubles navigating their way around new cities. Some people deal with this problem by printing out maps of all of the places they intend to go before they leave. Others rely on inconsistent Wi-Fi to find the directions to their next location. Countries often have local transportation options that are unknown to tourists, and people have different priorities when it comes to picking forms of transporation. Our goal is to provide optimal local transit suggestions to travelers based on their priorities.


Our Solution

An interactive display for on-the-go planning

Many different transportation options and priority filters to choose from

Detailed route guidance and reviews from other travelers

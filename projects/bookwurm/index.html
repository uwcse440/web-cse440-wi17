BookWurm adds digital conveniences to physical reading.

Find out how

Track your reading.
Figure out what to read next.

By using BookWurm's smart bookmark, tracking reading sessions is made easy. Simply snap the bookmark onto your wrist to begin tracking. The smart bookmark automatically tracks how many pages you read based on your wrist movements.

Once you remove the bookmark from your wrist, information about your reading session is automatically transferred to the companion app.

You can check out your reading stats on the application's dashboard.

You can set goals related to your reading and get suggestions on what kinds of books to start reading next based on what you've read and what other people have found interesting.

Features

Tools to enhance your reading experience

Identify reading habits

Check out graphs of your reading habits to understand when you read most productively.

Easily scan in book info

Scan in book information by scanning the book's barcode. Manual information entry and ISBN entry are also supported.

Suggestions and goals

Create a list of book goals either by manual entry or by selecting from a list of recommendations.

Do not disturb mode

Option to silence all notifications from your phone with the touch of a button to promote a distraction free reading experience.

OVERVIEW

Problem

Mental wellness is a self-defined state of wellness that differs from mental health, which is diagnosable by a professional.Two critical components in maintaining mental wellness are self reflection and social interaction. Many people often do not take the time to regularly perform conscious self reflection or meaningful social interaction, particularly with the widespread use of social media.

Solution

Wishing Well supports people in self reflection and social interaction with more purpose and meaning. We encourage self reflection with regular, customizable journaling and a color coded calendar view. We also facilitate purposeful interaction with a small group of friends through viewing their entries and a button to ping a friend when you want to let them know you are checking on them.
